Stimpatico LLC is building a safer and friendlier world for neurodivergent people.

We are headquartered in rain-soaked Washington State. We were founded in 2025 by [Andrew Merenbach](https://www.merenbach.com) and [Elizabeth Cheney](https://www.lizcheney.com).

Andrew has more than a decade in the technology industry, spanning software engineering, system administration, and information security. He is also neurodivergent and a proponent of Nonviolent Communication.

Elizabeth has more than a decade of information literacy expertise.

Stimpatico LLC is a Washington State Limited Liability Company. We're looking forward to working with you. Email us at `info@stimpatico.com`.
